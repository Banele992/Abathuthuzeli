About Abathuthuzeli

A proudly South African company committed to providing quality funeral services and financial solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 1999 in Phalaborwa, Two Mountains started as a unique entrepreneurial company that manufactured coffins. The company was established with a vision to serve communities with affordable funeral cover and quality funeral services.

Today, with over 90 branches across 5 provinces and more than 800 employees, Two Mountains has grown into one of South Africa's most trusted funeral service providers.
